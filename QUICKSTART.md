## First Steps in the Application

1. **Explore the Welcome Project**
   - Look at the left sidebar to see the file tree
   - Click on `hello.js` or `calculator.js` to open them

2. **Run Your First Code**
   - Click the "Run Code" button in the toolbar
   - See the output in the right panel

3. **Create Your Own Project**
   - Click the "+" button next to "Projects"
   - Enter a name and description
   - Start coding!
